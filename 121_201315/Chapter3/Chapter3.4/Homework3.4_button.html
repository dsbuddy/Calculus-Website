EXPECTED SKILLS:

  • Be able to solve related rates problems. It may be helpful to remember the following strategy:

    1. Read the problem carefully.

    2. Draw a diagram, if possible, representing the situation at an arbitrary time \(t\).

    3. Introduce notation, making sure to assign symbols to all quantities which are functions of time.

    4. Express the given information and the rate that you are trying to find in terms of derivatives.

    5. Write an equation which relates the various quantities of the problem.

    6. Differentiate both sides with respect to \(t\). (Keep in mind which quantites are functions of \(t\) and which quantities are remaining constant.)

    7. Substitute all of the relevant information into the resulting equation and solve for the the unknown rate.
