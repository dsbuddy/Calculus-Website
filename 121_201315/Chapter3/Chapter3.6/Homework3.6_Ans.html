EXPECTED SKILLS:

  • Know how to use L’Hopital’s Rule to help compute limits involving indeterminate forms of \(\frac{0}{0}\) and \(\frac{\infty}{\infty}\)

  • Be able to compute limits involving indeterminate forms \(\infty-\infty\), \(0 \cdot \infty\), \(0^0\), \(\infty^0\), and \(1^{\infty}\) by manipulating the limits into a form where L’Hopital’s Rule is applicable.
