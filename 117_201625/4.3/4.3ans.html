Expected Skills:

  • Be able to define \(\sinh{x}\) and \(\cosh{x}\) in terms of exponential functions.

  • Be able to determine the domain, range, and graph of \(\sinh{x}\) and \(\cosh{x}\).

  • Be able to justify properties and solve equations involving the hyperbolic functions.

  • Be able to compute limits and derivatives involving the hyperbolic functions.

Practice Problems:

  1. Consider \(f(x)=\sinh{x}\).

    1. Compute \(\lim_{x \rightarrow \infty}f(x)\) and \(\lim_{x \rightarrow -\infty}f(x)\)

    2. Determine whether the graph of \(f(x)\) has any curvilinear asymptotes.

    3. Compute the \(x\) and \(y\) intercepts of \(f(x)\).

    4. Solve \(\sinh{x}=1\) for \(x\).

    5. Show that \(\frac{d}{dx}(\sinh{x})=\cosh{x}\)

    6. Find all intervals on which \(f(x)\) is increasing and those on which \(f(x)\) is decreasing.

    7. Find all intervals on which \(f(x)\) is concave up and those on which \(f(x)\) is concave down.

    8. Determine the coordinates of all local extrema (max/min) and all inflection points.

    9. Sketch the graph of \(f(x)\)

  2. In order to verify the identity \(\sinh{2x}=2\sinh{x}\cosh{x}\) compute the following by appealing to the appropriate definitions.

    1. \(\sinh{2x}\)

    2. \(2\sinh{x}\cosh{x}\)

  3. We define the Hyperbolic Tangent function to be \(f(x)=\tanh{x}=\frac{\sinh{x}}{\cosh{x}}\).

    1. Express \(f(x)\) in terms of exponential functions.

    2. What is the domain of \(f(x)\)?

    3. Compute \(\lim_{x\to\infty}f(x)\) and \(\lim_{x\to-\infty}f(x)\).

    4. Determine whether the graph of \(f(x)\) has any curvilinear asymptotes.

    5. Find the coordinates of all \(x\) and \(y\) intercepts of \(f(x)\).

    6. Find all \(x\) for which \(f(x)=\frac{1}{2}\).

  4. Compute an equation of the line which is tangent to \(f(x)=\cosh{x}\) at the point where \(x=\ln{2}\).

  5. Suppose \(y=x\cosh{x}\). Compute \(\frac{d^2y}{dx^2}\).
