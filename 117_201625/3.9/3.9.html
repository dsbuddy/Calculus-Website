Expected Skills:

  • Be able to specify the domain and range of \(\sin^{-1}(x)\), \(\cos^{-1}(x)\), and \(\tan^{-1}(x)\). Also be able to graph these functions.

  • Be able to evaluate an inverse trigonometric function at a ratio which is related to the common angles of \(0^\circ-30^\circ-45^\circ-60^\circ-90^\circ\).

  • Be able to evaluate limits involving inverse trigonometric functions.

  • Be able to differentiate \(\sin^{-1}(x)\), \(\cos^{-1}(x)\), and \(\tan^{-1}(x)\). Also be able to use the derivative to solve application problems.

  6. For each of the following, find all solutions in the interval \([0,2\pi]\). Give the exact values, not decimal approximations.

    1. \((\sin{x}-1)(4\sin{x}-3) = 0\)

    2. \(3\tan{x}=1\)

    3. \(5\cos^2{x}+11\cos{x}+2=0\)

    4. \(3\tan{x}=-1\)

  7. Evaluate the following limits. If a limit does not exist, write \(+\infty\), \(-\infty\), or DNE.

    1. \(\displaystyle \lim_{x\rightarrow \infty}{\arccos{\left(\frac{-x^2}{x^2+3x}\right)}}\)

    2. \(\displaystyle \lim_{x\rightarrow 0}{\arctan{\left(\frac{1}{x^2}\right)}}\)

    3. \(\lim_{h \rightarrow 0}{\frac{\sin^{-1}{\left(\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}+h\right)}-\frac{\pi}{3}}{h}}\)
      (Hint: Interpreting the limit as the derivative of a function a particular point.)

  8. Calculate \(\frac{dy}{dx}\)

    1. \(y=\left(\tan^{-1}x\right)^3\)

    2. \(y=3x^2\sin^{-1}(4x)\)

  9. Compute an equation of the line which is tangent to the graph of \(f(x)=\cos^{-1}{x}\) at the point where \(x=\frac{1}{2}\).

  10. Find all value(s) of \(x\) at which the tangent lines to the graph of \(f(x)=\tan^{-1}{(4x)}\) are perpendicular to the line which passes through \((0,1)\) and \((2,0)\).

  11. Let \(f(x)=\arctan{x^2}\).

    4. Locate all points of inflection. Express each as an ordered pair \((x,y)\).

    5. Sketch \(f(x)\).
