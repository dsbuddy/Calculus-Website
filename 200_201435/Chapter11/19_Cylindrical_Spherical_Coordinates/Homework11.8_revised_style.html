For problems 5-10, each of the given surfaces is expressed in rectangular coordinates. Express the equation of the surface in (a) cylindrical coordinates and (b) spherical coordinates.

  1. \(x^2+y^2+z^2=16\)



    (a)\(r^2+z^2=16\); (b) \(\rho=4\)

  2. \(x^2+y^2+z^2=3z\)



    (a)\(r^2+z^2=3z\); (b) \(\rho=3\cos{\phi}\)

  3. \(z=\sqrt{2x^2+2y^2}\)



    (a)\(z=\sqrt{2}r\); (b) \(\phi=\cos^{-1}\left(\frac{2}{\sqrt{6}}\right)\)

  4. \(x^2+y^2=9\)



    (a)\(r=3\); (b) \(\rho\sin{\phi}=3\)

  5. \(x+3y+5z=4\)



    (a)\(r\cos{\theta}+3r\sin{\theta}+5z=4\); (b) \(\rho\cos{\theta}\sin{\phi}+3\rho\sin{\theta}\sin{\phi}+5\rho\cos{\phi}=4\)

  6. \(z=2\)



    (a)\(z=2\); (b) \(\rho\cos{\phi}=2\)
