  • Determine limits at infinity.

  • Evaluate integrals using various techniques such as substitution, parts, and partial fractions.

  • Evaluate improper integrals using limits.

PRACTICE PROBLEMS:

For problems 1 - 10, evaluate the limit.

  1. \(\lim_{x \to +\infty}\left({\frac{50000}{x}}\right)\)

    \(0\)

  2. \(\lim_{x \to +\infty}\left({\frac{4x-3x^5}{2x^5+4x^3+x^2+5}}\right)\)

    \(-\frac{3}{2}\)

  3. \(\lim_{x \to +\infty}\left({\frac{\sqrt{4+3x^2}}{2+7x}}\right)\)

    \(\frac{\sqrt{3}}{7}\)

  4. \(\lim_{x \to +\infty}\left({\sqrt{x^2+8x-5}-x}\right)\)

    \(4\)

  5. \(\lim_{x \to +\infty}\left({\frac{e^x-e^{-x}}{e^x+e^{-x}}}\right)\)

    \(1\)

  6. \(\lim_{x \to +\infty}\left({xe^{-x}}\right)\)

    \(0\)

  7. \(\lim_{x \to +\infty}\left({\frac{\cos{x}}{x}}\right)\)

    \(0\)

  8. \(\lim_{x \to +\infty}\left({\arctan\left(\frac{1}{x}\right)-\arctan(x)}\right)\)

    \(-\frac{\pi}{2}\)

  9. \(\lim_{x \to +\infty}\left(1+\frac{1}{x}\right)^{x}\)

    \(e\)

  10. \(\lim_{x \to +\infty}\left(1+3^x\right)^{1/x}\)

    \(3\)

For problems 11 - 22, evaluate the integral. If the integral is improper, determine what it converges to or show that it diverges.

  1. \(\int^e_{\sqrt{e}} \frac{1}{4x} \,dx\)

    \(\frac{1}{8}\)

  2. \(\int \frac{\sqrt{\ln{x}}}{x} \,dx\)

    \(\frac{2}{3}\left(\ln{x}\right)^{3/2}+C\)

  3. \(\int^{\sqrt{\pi}}_{0} x\sin(x^2) \,dx\)

    \(1\)

  4. \(\int x^2 \sin{x} \,dx\)

    \(2x\sin{x}-x^2\cos{x}+2\cos{x}+C\)

  5. \(\int \tan{x} \,dx\)

    \(-\ln{|\cos{x}|}+C=\ln|\sec{x}|+C\)

  6. \(\int \frac{1}{x^2+9} \,dx\)

    \(\frac{1}{3}\arctan\left(\frac{x}{3}\right)+C\)

  7. \(\int^{+\infty}_1 \frac{x}{x^2+9} \,dx\)

    Diverges to \(+\infty\)

  8. \(\int \frac{dx}{x^3-x}\)

    \(\frac{1}{2}\ln|x-1|+\frac{1}{2}\ln|x+1|-\ln|x|+C\)

  9. \(\int^{1/2}_0 \frac{3}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} \,dx\)

    \(\frac{\pi}{2}\)

  10. \(\int_1^{+\infty} xe^{-x} \,dx\)

    Converges to \(\frac{2}{e}\)

  11. \(\int \sqrt{x}\ln{x} \,dx\)

    \(\frac{2}{3}x^{3/2}\ln{x}-\frac{4}{9}x^{3/2}+C\)

  12. \(\int_5^{+\infty} \frac{dx}{x^2-3x-4}\)

    Converges to \(\frac{1}{5}\ln{6}\)
